    term: Avian Influenza
    definition: >-
      Avian influenza is an infectious disease of birds caused by type A
      influenza viruses of the Orthomyxoviridae family. Naturally occurring
      among wild bird populations, avian influenza viruses can infect domestic
      poultry and other bird species. Some avian influenza viruses can also
      infect mammals and those affecting humans are called zoonotic. A pandemic
      can occur when a novel influenza virus spreads in human populations
      worldwide (adapted from FAO, 2009; WHO, 2023a; 2023b; WOAH, no date).
