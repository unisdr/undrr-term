    term: Stampede or Crushing (Human)
    definition: >-
      Stampede or crushing is the surge of individuals in a crowd, in response
      to real or perceived danger or loss of physical space. It often disrupts
      the orderly movement of crowds resulting in movement for self-protection
      leading to increased localised crowd density and physical compression of
      the human bodies (Adapted from Burkle & Hsu, 2011; Illiyas et al., 2013;
      Ngai, et al., 2009).
