    term: Aflatoxins
    definition: >-
      Aflatoxins are mycotoxins – toxic compounds that are naturally produced by
      certain types of mould (fungi). Aflatoxins are among the most poisonous
      mycotoxins and are produced by certain moulds (Aspergillus flavus and A.
      parasiticus) that grow in soil, decaying vegetation, hay, and grains.
      Aflatoxins pose a serious health risk to humans and livestock (WHO, 2023).
