    term: River Erosion and Accretion
    definition: >-
      River erosion is the removal of material from the banks and beds of rivers
      and streams (cf. Lawler, 1993). River accretion is the formation of new
      land such as channel bars, sandbanks and deltas by sedimentation or
      changing river flow (after Islam and Guchhait, 2020 and Hasanuzzaman et
      al., 2024).
