    description: >-
      Additional definitions of thaw include:

      To melt a substance, ice for example, by warming it to a temperature
      greater than the melting point of the substance, or to have frozen
      contents melted (AMS, 2012).

      To free something from the binding action of ice by warming it to a
      temperature above the melting point of ice (AMS, 2012). A warm spell when
      ice and snow melt, for example, 'January thaw' (AMS, 2012).
