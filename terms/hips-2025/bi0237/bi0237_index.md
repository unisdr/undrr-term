    term: Tuberculosis
    definition: >-
      Tuberculosis (TB) is a curable bacterial infectious disease caused by
      Mycobacterium tuberculosis that most commonly affects the lungs. It causes
      national epidemics of varied severity worldwide. Forms of TB that are
      resistant to treatment - multi-drug-resistant TB (MDR-TB) and extensively
      drug-resistant TB (XDR-TB) - are public health crises and threaten health
      security worldwide (WHO, 2024).
