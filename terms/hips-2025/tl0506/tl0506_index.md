    term: Marine Debris
    definition: >-
      Marine debris is any persistent, manufactured or processed solid material
      discarded, disposed of or abandoned in the marine and coastal environment.
      Marine litter consists of items that have been made or used by people and
      deliberately discarded into the sea or rivers or on beaches; brought
      indirectly to the sea with rivers, sewage, stormwater or winds; or
      accidentally lost, including material lost at sea in bad weather (adapted
      from UN Environment, no date and NOAA, no date).
