  en:
    term: Biodiversity Loss
    definition: >-
      Biodiversity loss refers to the reduction of any aspect of biological
      diversity (i.e. diversity at the genetic, species and ecosystem levels) in
      a particular area through death (including extinction), destruction or
      manual removal. It can occur at many scales, from global extinctions to
      local population extinctions, leading to a decline in total diversity at
      the same scale.
