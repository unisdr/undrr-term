    term: Forest Disturbances
    definition: >-
      Forest disturbance is the damage caused by any factor (biotic or abiotic)
      that adversely affects the vigour and productivity of the forest, and
      which is not a direct result of human activities. It includes disturbance
      by insect pests, diseases, severe weather events and fires (FAO, 2018;
      2020).
