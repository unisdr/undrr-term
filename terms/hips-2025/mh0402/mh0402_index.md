    term: Rain
    definition: >-
      Rain is precipitation of drops of water that falls from a cloud (WMO,
      2017). While rain is essential for sustaining life and ecosystems, extreme
      rainfall is a primary trigger for some of the most devastating secondary
      hazards—flooding, landslides, and soil erosion—which result in widespread
      loss of life, damage to infrastructure, disruption of livelihoods, and
      environmental degradation (Rijal et al., 2024; Myhre et al., 2019).
