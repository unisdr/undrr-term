    term: Human-Wildlife Conflict
    definition: >-

      presence or behaviour of wildlife poses an actual or perceived, direct and
      recurring threat to human interests or needs, leading to disagreements
      between groups of people and negative impacts on people and/or wildlife
      (IUCN SSC, 2022).
