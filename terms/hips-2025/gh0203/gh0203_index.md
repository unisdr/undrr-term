  en:
    term: Pyroclastic Density Current
    definition: >-
      Pyroclastic density currents are hot, fast-moving mixtures of volcanic
      particles and gas that flow according to their density relative to the
      surrounding medium and the Earth's gravity. They typically originate from
      the gravitational collapse of explosive eruption columns, lava domes or
      lava-flow fronts, and explosive lateral blasts (cf. Charbonnier et al.,
      2025).
