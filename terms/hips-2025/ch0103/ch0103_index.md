    term: Lead
    definition: >-
      Lead is a naturally occurring highly toxic heavy metal. Its widespread use
      has caused extensive environmental contamination and health problems in
      many parts of the world. It is a cumulative toxicant that affects multiple
      body systems, including the neurological, haematological,
      gastrointestinal, cardiovascular and renal systems. Children are
      particularly vulnerable to the neurotoxic effects of lead, and even
      relatively low levels of exposure can cause serious and, in some cases,
      irreversible neurological damage (WHO, 2024).
