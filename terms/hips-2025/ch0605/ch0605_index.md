    term: Marine Toxins
    definition: >-
      Marine toxins (biotoxins) are naturally occurring, toxic substances,
      mostly caused by certain types of marine organisms such as toxic algae,
      but also by bacteria. These toxins can accumulate in fish and shellfish,
      causing significant public health concerns due to their potential to cause
      a wide range of adverse health effects.
