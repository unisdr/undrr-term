    term: Ocean Acidification
    definition: >-
      Ocean acidification refers to a reduction in the pH of the ocean over an
      extended period, caused primarily by the uptake of carbon dioxide from the
      atmosphere. It can also result from other chemical additions to or
      subtractions from the ocean (IPCC, 2011).
