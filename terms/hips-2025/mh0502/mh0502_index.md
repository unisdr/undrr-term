    term: Cold Wave
    definition: >-
      A cold wave is a period of marked and unusual cold weather characterised
      by a sharp and significant drop in air temperatures near the surface
      (maximum, minimum and daily average) over a large area and persisting
      below certain thresholds for at least two consecutive days during the cold
      season (WMO, 2020).
