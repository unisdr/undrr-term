    term: 'Viral, Phytoplasma and Viroid Plant Disease Outbreaks'
    definition: >-
      Viral, phytoplasma and viroid plant disease outbreaks refer to sudden
      occurrence of plant diseases caused by viruses, phytoplasma (syn.
      mycoplasma-like organisms) and viroids over large areas with significant
      impact on crop production or natural habitats (adapted from Nakashima &
      Murata, 1993; Hammond & Owens, 2006; FAO / IPPC, 2016; Rubio et al.,
      2020).
