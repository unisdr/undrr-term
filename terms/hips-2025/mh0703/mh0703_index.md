    term: Storm Surge
    definition: >-
      A storm surge reflects the difference between the actual water level under
      the influence of a meteorological disturbance (storm tide) and the level
      which would have occurred in the absence of the meteorological disturbance
      (i.e., astronomical tide) (WMO, 2008, 2011, 2017).
