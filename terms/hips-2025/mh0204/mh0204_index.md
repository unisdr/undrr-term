  en:
    term: Sand haze
    definition: >-
      A dust haze (or sand haze) is a suspension in the air of dust or small
      sand particles, raised from the ground prior to the time of observation by
      a dust storm or sandstorm. The dust storm or sandstorm may have occurred
      either at or near the observation site or far from it (WMO, 2025).
