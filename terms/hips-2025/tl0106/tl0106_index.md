    term: Cyberbullying
    definition: >-
      Cyberbullying is bullying that takes place using digital devices such as
      cell/mobile phones, computers, and tablets. Cyberbullying can occur
      through SMS, e-mail, apps, social media, forums, or gaming when people
      view, participate in, or share content. Cyberbullying includes the
      deliberate sending, posting, or sharing of negative, harmful, false, or
      mean content about someone else. It can include sharing personal or
      private information about someone else causing embarrassment or
      humiliation. Some cyberbullying may also be unlawful or criminal behaviour
      (adapted from UNICEF, no date; PHE, 2014; US Government, no date).
