    term: Radioactive Agents & Material
    definition: >-
      A substance or a material emitting, or related to the emission of,
      ionizing radiation (either in the form of electro-magnetic waves or
      particle radiation) is radioactive. Depending on the magnitude of
      exposure, the radioactive substance may affect human health; as such it is
      subject to regulatory control by national laws and national regulatory
      authorities. Radioactive material may also be a hazard to animal health,
      other forms of life and the environment (IAEA, 2018).
