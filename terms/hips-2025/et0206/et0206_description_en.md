Space debris results from:
- Release during normal operations of spacecraft in outer space,
- Accidental break-ups of spacecraft during operational phases,
- Accidental collisions during operations to place spacecraft in outer space, or when spacecraft are already in outer space,
- Intentional destruction of any on-orbit spacecraft and launch vehicle orbital stages or other harmful activities that generate long-lived debris,
- Post-mission break-ups resulting from stored energy of spacecraft (UNOOSA, 2007, COPUOS, 2024).
