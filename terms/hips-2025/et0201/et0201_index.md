    term: Airburst
    definition: >-
      An airburst is defined as a high-energy explosion in mid-air caused either
      by an artificial explosive device or by the sudden disruption of a natural
      object entering the Earth's atmosphere at high speeds at an altitude where
      the hydrodynamic pressure exceeds the structural integrity of the object
      (adapted from ESA, No date).

      An airburst is defined as an explosion in the air, especially of a nuclear
      bomb or large meteorite (Lexico Dictionary, no date).
