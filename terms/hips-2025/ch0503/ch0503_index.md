    term: Perfluoroalkyl and Polyfluoroalkyl Substances
    definition: >-
      Polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS) are a large family of chemicals in which
      multiple fluorine atoms are attached to an alkyl chain within a molecule .
      If all possible sites of an alkyl chain are occupied by fluorine atoms,
      the substance may also be referred to as a perfluoroalkyl substance as in
      perfluorooctanoic acid (PFOA) or perfluorooctanesulfonic acid (PFOS).
      (WHO, 2023).
