  en:
    term: Depression or Cyclone (Low Pressure Area)
    definition: >-
      A depression or cyclone is a region of the atmosphere in which the
      pressure is lower than that of the surrounding region at the same level
      (WMO, 1992).
    source:
      text: 'UNDRR & ISC, 2025'
      url: 'https://www.preventionweb.net/hips'
    aliases:
      - Low-pressure area
    description: >-
      A depression or cyclone is represented on a synoptic chart by a system of
      isobars at a specified altitude level (or a system of contours at a
      specified pressure level) which enclose relatively low values of pressure
      (or altitude) (WMO, 1992).
