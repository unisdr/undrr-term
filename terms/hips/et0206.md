  en:
    term: Space Debris
    definition: >-
      Space debris refers to all human-made objects, including fragments and
      elements thereof, in Earth orbit or re-entering the atmosphere, that are
      nonfunctional (COPUOS, 2002 and UNOOSA, 2007).
    source:
      text: 'UNDRR & ISC, 2025'
      url: 'https://www.preventionweb.net/hips'
    aliases:
      - Orbital Debris
      - Space litter
    description: >-
      Space debris results from:

      - Release during normal operations of spacecraft in outer space,

      - Accidental break-ups of spacecraft during operational phases,

      - Accidental collisions during operations to place spacecraft in outer
      space, or when spacecraft are already in outer space,

      - Intentional destruction of any on-orbit spacecraft and launch vehicle
      orbital stages or other harmful activities that generate long-lived
      debris,

      - Post-mission break-ups resulting from stored energy of spacecraft
      (UNOOSA, 2007, COPUOS, 2024).
